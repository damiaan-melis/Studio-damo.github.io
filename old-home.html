We Can Win
We Can Win
 / 6

We Can Win combines the strengths of grassroots community organising with forward-thinking digital and creative communications. We are organising in local communities to find and train a new generation of progressive community leaders. We have also run campaigns against air pollution in Central London and the problem of undemocratic Business votes in the City Of London Corporation.

Progressive Alliance
Progressive Alliance
 / 6

The Progressive Alliance is a movement for a better, less tribalistic politics, where progressive people and parties work together to create the change we want to see in society - greater equality, democracy and sustainability.

We recruited 100K tactical voters and raised nearly ¬£200K in just 7 weeks. Progressive Alliance thwarted the Conservatives in 22 of our 31 target seats and ended their Parliamentary majority.

Meliora Journal I-IV
Meliora Journal I-IV
 / 6

Meliora Journals are self-published, not-for-profit, multi-authored publications that explore the intersections between art and activism. Each publication is the outcome of a collaborative public workshop. They act to bring people together, including those who would not otherwise have met and therefore attempt to start a conversation, a think tank for change inspired by public expression and community.

Collage Generator
Collage Generator
 / 6

An on-going self-directed experiment to teach myself the visual programming language processing. Exploring a personal fascination with visual computation / semi-autonomous image generation and appropriation. It poses the question: Can we create spontaneous visual meaning by cutting up and randomly combining a selection of images?

All imagery is sourced from the exhaustive public image archives of the creative commons.
